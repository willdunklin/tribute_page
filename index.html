Insight (2018)
is based on the album art for "Awaken, My Love!" by Donald Glover.

As part of an exhibit at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, this piece is a figure study painted on newspaper.

Created using a just pen and a piece of paper, simplicity is often beautiful.

Beccah Dunklin
is an aspiring young artist from Buffalo, NY. She attends Buffalo Seminary where she creates her artwork. She is highly involved in many mediums of art such as theater, digital and physical forms. She is currently producing commissioned pieces for customers while studying, growing and refining her skills.
